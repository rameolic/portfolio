My Story

I'm Ramkumar Koppula, a Mobile Application Developer with over 5+ years of hands-on experience building, testing, deploying, and maintaining cross-platform apps using Flutter. Since December 2020, I've been the sole mobile developer at a fast-paced startup, leading mobile development across three major products — from setting up architecture to final deployment. Working closely with a team of backend engineers and testers, I handle everything from UI implementation, code reviews, API integration, bug fixing, and app distribution. I've led complete mobile builds, participated in daily product iterations, and ensured on-time delivery with reliable performance. My work has been recognized through equity in the company, reflecting my long-term value. While I haven’t received formal PF/UAN documentation due to startup scale, I maintain complete experience proof through internal agreements, share transfer documents, and public product releases. My tech stack includes Flutter, Dart, Firebase, REST APIs, Git, GitHub, and Postman. I’m also familiar with backend frameworks like PHP and CodeIgniter, and I regularly collaborate across frontend and backend teams to build seamless app experiences. I enjoy mentoring and have supported interns early in my career, some of whom now work at leading MNCs. With multiple apps shipped to the Play Store and internal clients, I aim to join a larger, structured team in the future — bringing with me the startup-level ownership, adaptability, and full-stack thinking that define my development style.
